### Description
The Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset contains real-world transactional data from a Brazilian online marketplace.  
It includes detailed information related to:
- Orders
- Customers
- Sellers
- Products
- Payments
- Reviews and delivery timelines

This dataset is well-suited for building **analytical data platforms** and simulating real-world retail data engineering use cases.

## ğŸ—„ï¸ Data Storage & Management Strategy

- **Raw Layer**
  - Original CSV files are stored in **Azure Data Lake Storage Gen2 (ADLS)**.
  - Data is preserved in its original format to maintain traceability and support reprocessing.

- **Bronze Layer**
  - Raw data is ingested into Azure Databricks and stored in **Delta Lake format**.
  - No transformations are applied at this stage.

- **Silver Layer**
  - Cleaned and standardized datasets are produced by applying:
    - Deduplication
    - Data validation
    - Schema enforcement
    - Type casting and normalization

- **Gold Layer**
  - Business and analytics-ready datasets are created using a **Star Schema**.
  - Fact and dimension tables are optimized for reporting and analytical queries.
